Shaniwar Wada has five gates, with the main entrance named Dilidarwaza. The second gate faces north, and it is named the Mastani gate. Obviously Mastani gate was named after the second wife of Bajirao This gate is named after her name because she used this gate to get into the Wada. Ten soldiers guarded this same gate.

The BAPS Swaminarayan Mandir in Pune is a magnificent Hindu temple dedicated to Bhagwan Swaminarayan, built and managed by the Bochasanwasi Akshar Purushottam Swaminarayan Sanstha (BAPS).The temple showcases traditional Nagara-style architecture, featuring intricately carved marble and sandstone structures that reflect rich cultural heritage.
